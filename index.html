About Us

We are the 01 Intake L/AIP 001/2019, a group of professionals dedicated to supporting each other and making a positive impact in our community. Our intake was established with the goal of creating lasting connections and fostering mutual growth.

We organize regular events, support member initiatives, and maintain an emergency fund to assist members in times of need.

Intake Projects

We're currently working on several initiatives to benefit our members and the wider community:

  • Professional Development Workshop Series
  • Community Outreach Program
  • Alumni Networking Platform
  • Mentorship Program for New Members

Intake Emergency Fund

Our emergency fund provides financial assistance to members facing unexpected hardships. The fund is supported by voluntary contributions from members and managed by a committee elected by the intake.

To apply for assistance or make a contribution, please contact the fund administrator through the contact form.
